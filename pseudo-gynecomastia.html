What is Pseudo-Gynecomastia?

Pseudo-gynecomastia, also known as "false gynecomastia" or lipomastia, is chest enlargement caused solely by excess fat deposits without any glandular tissue involvement. Unlike true gynecomastia, there is no firm breast tissue present.

This condition is commonly seen in overweight or obese men and can persist even after significant weight loss. The excess fat creates a breast-like appearance that can be just as emotionally distressing as true gynecomastia.

Key Difference: Pseudo-gynecomastia feels soft and fatty, while true gynecomastia has a firm, rubbery texture beneath the nipple.

  • Avoid significant weight fluctuations
