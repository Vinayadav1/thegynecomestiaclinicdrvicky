What is Scarless Surgery?

"Scarless" gynecomastia surgery refers to advanced surgical techniques that minimize visible scarring through tiny, strategically placed incisions. While no surgery is truly scarless, Dr. Jain's approach results in scars so minimal they're virtually undetectable.

Using state-of-the-art ultrasound technology and meticulous surgical technique, Dr. Jain performs gynecomastia correction through incisions as small as 3-4mm, hidden in natural skin creases or the areola border.

Dr. Jain's Promise: Scars so minimal, you'll forget you had surgery. Most patients report their scars fade to nearly invisible within 6-12 months.

Advanced Scarless Techniques

Advanced Techniques

For Grade 1 and pseudo-gynecomastia cases, tiny 3-4mm incisions are all that's needed.

  • Incisions smaller than a grain of rice
  • Hidden in natural skin folds
  • Fade to nearly invisible
  • No stitches required

Periareolar Incision

For cases requiring gland excision, incisions are placed along the areola border where they blend naturally.

  • Follows natural color transition
  • Camouflaged by pigmentation
  • Minimal length (2-3cm)
  • Excellent healing properties

Post-Operative Scar Care

First 2 Weeks

  • Keep incisions clean and dry
  • Follow wound care instructions
  • Avoid sun exposure
  • Wear compression garment

Weeks 2-12

  • Apply silicone gel or sheets
  • Gentle scar massage
  • Use sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Attend follow-up appointments

Scar Healing Timeline

2W

Incisions closed and healing. Scars appear red or pink.
