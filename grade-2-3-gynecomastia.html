Grade 2 & 3 Gynecomastia

Comprehensive Treatment for Moderate to Severe Cases

II

Grade 2

Moderate Enlargement

Moderate breast enlargement extending beyond the areola with edges that are indistinct from the chest. Requires combination treatment of liposuction and gland excision.

III

Grade 3

Moderate to Severe

Moderate to severe enlargement with excess skin. May require skin excision in addition to liposuction and gland removal for optimal chest contour.

Understanding Your Condition

Grade 2 and 3 gynecomastia represent moderate to severe cases where both glandular tissue and excess fat contribute to breast enlargement. These grades require a more comprehensive surgical approach to achieve optimal results.

The key difference between Grade 2 and 3 is the presence of excess skin. Grade 3 cases often require additional skin tightening procedures to achieve a masculine chest contour.

Important: These grades benefit most from Dr. Jain's expertise in combining multiple techniques for natural-looking results.
