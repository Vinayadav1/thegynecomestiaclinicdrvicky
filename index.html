ABOUT

The Gynecomastia Clinic is India's premier destination for male breast reduction surgery. Founded by Dr. Vicky Jain, we specialize exclusively in gynecomastia treatment, offering world-class surgical solutions with a focus on natural results and patient satisfaction.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines advanced surgical techniques with compassionate care, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention throughout their transformation journey. With over 2000 successful surgeries and a 98% satisfaction rate, we have established ourselves as the trusted choice for gynecomastia treatment in India. Our commitment to excellence, safety, and patient care sets us apart as leaders in the field.
