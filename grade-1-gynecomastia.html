Grade I Gynecomastia

What is Grade 1 Gynecomastia?

Grade 1 gynecomastia is the mildest form of male breast enlargement, characterized by small localized button of tissue beneath the nipple area. There is minimal breast enlargement and no excess skin.

This grade typically affects younger men and is often the easiest to treat with the most minimal surgical intervention. The condition is confined to the areolar region without extending beyond the areola borders.

Good News: Grade 1 gynecomastia responds excellently to treatment with minimal scarring and quick recovery time.

Treatment Approach

Grade 1 gynecomastia is typically treated with liposuction alone, making it the least invasive option.
